about us

Welcome to our brand! We are more than just a totebag and accessories company; we are a lifestyle brand that celebrates style, functionality, and self-expression. Here's an overview of who we are and what we stand for:

At Planet-First, we believe that every accessory tells a story and adds a touch of personality to your everyday life. Our mission is to create beautifully crafted totebags and accessories that not only elevate your style but also serve as practical companions for your daily adventures.

Craftsmanship and quality are at the heart of everything we do. Each of our products is meticulously designed and crafted with utmost care, ensuring that it withstands the test of time. We work with skilled artisans and use premium materials to ensure durability, functionality, and impeccable finishing touches. From the stitching to the hardware, every detail is thoughtfully considered.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability. We understand the importance of minimizing our impact on the environment, and that's why we strive to use eco-friendly materials and practices throughout our supply chain. From utilizing recycled fabrics to exploring innovative sustainable materials, we actively seek ways to reduce our carbon footprint and contribute to a greener future.

Our designs are inspired by the ever-evolving world of fashion and art. We blend timeless elements with contemporary trends to create pieces that are both classic and on-trend. Whether you're looking for a sleek and minimalist totebag or a vibrant and playful accessory to make a statement, we have something to suit your individual style.
